About Me

Hi, I'm Mihran, known as Miran. I'm a 19-year-old guy based in Halabja/Sulaymaniyah. I'm currently studying IT at the Polytechnic University of Sulaymaniyah and am in my second year. I'm a 3D artist and a hobbyist photographer. I've been doing photography for as long as I can remember, mostly street, wildlife, and nature photography. I'm also a portrait photographer(forcefully), and I use a Sony a6000 with a 20mm f/2.8 and a Sony 55-210mm f/4.5-6.3 lens. However, most of the photos you see were taken with my phone. I hope my work inspires you and gives you some ideas about this world.
